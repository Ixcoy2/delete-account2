How to Delete Your Account - Buho Conductores

You can delete your account and associated data using one of the following methods:

  • Within the app: Go to Settings > Delete Account. Follow the prompts to permanently delete your account.
  • Via email: Send a request to soportebuho5@gmail.com with your account details. We will process your request and delete your account and associated data.

Data that will be deleted:

  • Account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Trip history
  • Payment and balance information
  • Chat messages

Data that may be retained: Some anonymized logs or analytics data may be retained for up to 6 months for app performance and security purposes.
